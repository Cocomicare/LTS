⚠️ If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 911 immediately. Do not rely on this app in an emergency.

Not Medical Advice

PLTS Care is a personal wellness tracking application. The information, alerts, trends, and outputs provided by this app do not constitute medical advice of any kind. Always seek the advice of your physician, transplant team, or other qualified health provider with any questions regarding your medical condition.

Not a Diagnostic Tool

This app does not diagnose any medical condition. Alerts and color-coded indicators are for personal awareness based on general reference ranges and user-defined thresholds only. They are not clinical diagnoses. An alert does not mean you have a medical problem — and no alert does not mean you are medically clear.

Accuracy of Data

All data is entered by you. The app cannot verify the accuracy of your entries or the calibration of your home devices. Trends and correlations displayed may be misleading if data is entered inconsistently. This app is not a substitute for laboratory testing or clinical examination.

Medications

Never adjust, start, stop, or change any medication — especially immunosuppressants — based on information displayed in this app without first consulting your healthcare provider. Immunosuppressant management requires precise clinical oversight.

Lab Results

Lab values displayed in this app are for personal tracking only. Clinical interpretation requires a qualified healthcare provider with full knowledge of your medical history. Do not make medical decisions based solely on lab values shown here.

Correlation ≠ Causation

Medication and symptom patterns displayed are statistical observations in your personal data only. They do not establish causal relationships. Any apparent associations must be evaluated by your healthcare team before any conclusions or actions are taken.

Post-Transplant Caution

Lung transplant recipients are medically complex patients. Health changes can be serious and progress rapidly. Any concerning changes — declining readings, new symptoms, fever, unusual weight gain — should be reported to your transplant team promptly. Do not wait for this app to alert you if you feel something is wrong.

No Emergency Services

This app is not an emergency service and cannot detect or respond to medical emergencies. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 911 immediately.

Hold Harmless

By using this app, you expressly agree to hold harmless and release the creators, developers, and contributors of PLTS Care from any and all claims, liability, damages, or expenses of any kind arising from your use of the app, including any health outcomes or medical decisions made in connection with information displayed here.

Privacy Policy
What data is collected, stored, and who can see it
›
Version 1.0 · Effective at account creation

What We Collect

PLTS Care collects only what you enter yourself: your name, email address (for login), and health tracking data including vital signs, spirometry readings, symptoms, lab results, medications, body measurements, and sputum logs. We do not collect any data passively from your device, browser, or location.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in your personal account on Supabase — a secure cloud database platform. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. No data is stored on your device or in your browser's local storage.

Who Can See Your Data

Only you can access your data. Row-level security policies in the database ensure that every query is filtered to your account ID — no other user can read, modify, or delete your records. The app developer (Richard) has no routine access to your personal health entries.

AI Features & Your Data

When you use the Clinical Assistant, ask for lab extraction, or use other AI-powered features, a summary of your recent readings is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. This data is sent securely and is not stored by Anthropic beyond what their standard API data retention policy provides. No identifiable information (name, email, date of birth) is included in these requests.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and real-time sync. Your email is stored here for login purposes.
  • Anthropic — AI responses for the Clinical Assistant and lab extraction features only.
  • Google Fonts — font files loaded from Google's CDN at render time. No personal data is shared.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties. We do not serve advertisements.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual entries within the app at any time. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact the app administrator. Account deletion removes all your health records from the database.

Children's Privacy

PLTS Care is not intended for use by individuals under 18. If you are under 18, do not create an account or enter data into this app.
